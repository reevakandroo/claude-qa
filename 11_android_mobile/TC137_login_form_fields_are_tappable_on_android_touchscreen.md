|-------|--------|

## Preconditions

- [**PC001**](../00_preconditions/PC001_app_loaded_at_login_url.md) — App is open at the login page; no user is logged in

## Execution Steps

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Tap the email field |
| 2 | Observe if the keyboard appears and focus is set |

Both fields respond to tap. On-screen keyboard appears. User can type credentials.
