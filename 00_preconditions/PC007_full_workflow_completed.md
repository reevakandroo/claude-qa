## Description

A complete end-to-end assessment has been run. The risk result is on screen and Export PDF is visible.

## Setup Steps

1. Complete [PC006](PC006_fresh_ecg_open_patient_form.md): Fresh ECG Open.
2. Enter **Patient ID:** PT1001 (or test-specific ID).
3. Enter **Patient Name:** John Doe (or test-specific name).
4. Enter **Age:** 45 (or test-specific age).
5. Select **Gender:** Male (or test-specific gender).
6. Click **"Get Risk Assessment"**.
7. Wait for result (up to 60 seconds).
8. Confirm the risk result (Low / Moderate / High) is displayed.
9. Confirm the **Export PDF** button is visible.
