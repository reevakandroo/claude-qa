# PC006: Fresh Unprocessed ECG Open — Patient Form Visible

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Precondition ID** | PC006 |
| **Title** | Fresh Unprocessed ECG Open — Patient Form Visible |
| **Summary** | A New ECG is open; patient details form is displayed with all fields editable |

## Description

The user has opened a New ECG entry and the patient details form is on screen.

## Setup Steps

1. Complete [PC005](PC005_unprocessed_ecg_available.md): Unprocessed ECG Available.
2. Click on a **"New ECG"** entry.
3. Wait for the patient form to load.
4. Confirm all four fields are visible and editable: **Patient ID**, **Patient Name**, **Age**, **Gender**.
